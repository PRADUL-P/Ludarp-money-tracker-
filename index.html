About LUDARP Money Tracker

This app keeps all data locally in your browser. It is a premium offline tracker designed for maximum privacy and security.

📥 Entry Page
Add Income/Expenses, set Categories, use Voice Input, and Split bills with group members instantly.
📊 Summary Page
Visual charts, Pie statistics by category, and detailed Daily/Monthly/Yearly history logs.
🏦 Accounts
Track bank balances, UPI apps, and cards in one place with automatic calculation of total net worth.
🤝 Dues Tracker
Manage 'I Owe' and 'They Owe Me'. Settlement tracking with account mapping and dates.
📋 Statement
Bank-style tabular view for audit. Export everything to Excel or professional PDF reports.
☁️ Sync & Backup
Connect to Google Sheets for cloud sync. All data encrypted and private to you.
