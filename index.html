✨ LUDARP Money Tracker Premium

A professional-grade, privacy-first financial companion. Every byte of your data stays on your device—no cloud prying, no ads, just pure management.

📊 Smart Dashboard
▼
Real-time expense trends, category pie charts, and filtered history logs.
💡 **Deep-Dive:** Use the Summary tab to visualize your financial health. Toggle the 'Month/Day' view to compare spending periods. The system automatically highlights budget-exceeding categories in red. Tap on the Charts themselves to see exact amounts per segment.
📥 Quick Entry
▼
Voice input, auto-filling presets (Prefills), and split-bill support.
💡 **Efficiency:** Use the Prefills button in the Entry tab to save items you repeat daily. When using Voice, speak clearly: 'Fifty rupees for coffee' for auto-recognition. Trip Dates are available for the Travel category if you're booking in advance.
🤝 Advanced Dues
▼
Powerful 'I Owe' & 'They Owe' engine with Search and Bank Mapping.
💡 **Debt Management:** Track exactly who owes you what. When marking as 'Received', the app asks for the Bank account and date to create a matching settlement entry. Use the 🔍 Filter to find old records or search by name.
⛽ Fuel & Petrol Price
▼
Track fuel economy (km/L) and log daily petrol prices (₹/L).
💡 **Cost Analysis:** By entering ODO readings and Liters refilled, the app calculates vehicle performance. Logging the Price per Litre allows you to see how fuel rate changes impact your monthly budget over time.
🏦 Net Worth
▼
Multi-account support for Banks, Wallets, and Cards.
💡 **Balance Tracking:** Add your accounts in the Accounts tab. As you spend or receive income, choose the source account. The system maintains a ledger for each, ensuring your bank balance always matches the app's record.
📋 Master Reports
▼
Export filtered data to Excel (XLSX) or professional PDF.
💡 **Accounting:** Use the Statement view to audit your logs. You can filter by any specific category (e.g. only 'Rent') across years. Export high-quality PDFs for proof or XLSX for advanced data analysis.
🗂️ Infinite Ordering
▼
Drag-and-drop to reorder navigation and tabs.
💡 **Customization:** Head to Settings -> Order. Use the ☰ handles to prioritize the features you use most. Want 'Dues' as your first tab? Just drag it to the top to redefine your app flow.
🔒 Privacy & Lock
▼
Optional Biometric or PIN lock with AES encryption.
💡 **Security:** Your data is strictly offline. Use the optional PIN or Fingerprint lock for an extra layer of privacy. Backups are exported as AES-Encrypted JSON files, keeping them safe even on external storage.
☁️ Cloud Sync (Optional)

Connect your private Google Sheets to keep your data synced across devices while maintaining 100% ownership.
